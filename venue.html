Copenhagen, Denmark – Civil Ceremony

Date & Location

Venue: TBC - Copenhagen Town Hall
Address: Rådhuspladsen 1, 1553 København, Denmark

Getting There

  • Fly into Copenhagen Airport (CPH) from major cities in the US, UK, or India.
  • Search flights on Skyscanner.
  • From airport to city:
    Train/Metro (15 mins) or Taxi (20–30 mins).

Accommodation

  • We'll be staying at Axel Guldsmeden.
  • Other great hotels nearby:
    • Scandic Palace Hotel (right next to City Hall)
    • Hotel SP34
    • Wakeup Copenhagen

Getting Around

  • Excellent public transport (Metro, buses)
  • Walkable and bike-friendly city
  • Recommended: Download the DOT Tickets app

Tips

  • Denmark is in the Schengen Area – some guests may need a visa
  • Weather: October averages 6–14°C (42–57°F)
  • Language: Most Danes speak English fluently
