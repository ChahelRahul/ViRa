Our Story

Victoria and Rahul met in 2022 in Aberdeen, Scotland. Both were newcomers to the UK, each arriving to pursue a Master’s degree and a bit of personal growth along the way.

They first connected through an international students’ group chat, bonding over a shared goal: finding a gym partner. On 25 August 2022, they met up for the first time at Aberdeen Sports Village (ASV). Even though Victoria had no real idea what Olympic weightlifting was, she gave it her best shot!

From that day on, they became regular gym buddies, and before long, they were spending time together outside the gym too. By January 2023, they had trained so consistently they both competed in their first Olympic Weightlifting competition together.

Their friendship turned into something deeper, built through shared workouts, late-night chats, travels across the UK, and cheering each other on through career shifts and life changes. They celebrated milestones, big and small, and became one another’s biggest supporters.

In March 2024, they moved to Edinburgh, where they now live together by the beach. And in January 2025, Rahul asked Victoria to marry him.

These days, they’re still working on their Olympic lifts (sometimes hitting PRs, sometimes missing) but always cheering each other on, in the gym and in life.

They’ve built something strong, joyful, and full of love and they can’t wait to keep growing together.
